Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

At Gamyartha, accessible from https://gamyartha.online, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Gamyartha and how we use it.

Log Files

Gamyartha follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.

Personal Data we collect

We collect essential personal data to provide our services, including:

  • Email address and name for account creation.
  • Financial transaction data you voluntarily input (expenses, incomes, budgets).
  • Chat history with our AI financial advisor.

How we use your data

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our website.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our website.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our website.
  • Send you emails relating to your account (e.g., due date reminders).

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your personal information. Your password is hashed and salted. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@gamyartha.online
